1, The training and testing data are stored in the folders "train" and "test" respectively. 
2, Each folder contains both the data of "sub features" and "time series data" as the inputs of CNN-F and CNN-T models.  
3, In both of the "sub_features" and "time_series_data" folders in "test", we save datasets in terms of various time intervals, you can choose to test the accuracy of data with different time intervals. 
4, When train or test these datasets, please change the path of data in the corresponding codes.
